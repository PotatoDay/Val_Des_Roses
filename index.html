Bed&Breakfast Val des Roses

Centrally located on a quiet street in the historic heart of Bruges ans steps from the Grand Place, shopping, restaurants, and Bruges most landmarks, Val des Roses has been a charming Bed and Breakfast since 2015.
Our warm and welcoming hospitality and clean guest rooms include free continental breakfast, free parking space and free WiFi.
